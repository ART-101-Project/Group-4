What is A.I.?

Artificial intelligence is technology that allows computers and machines to engage in human intelligence in order to accomplish problem solving. Emerging as a field of computer science, AI seeks to process data information while recognizing patterns and making predictions/decisions modeled after human judgment. Linked to machine and deep learning, artificial intelligence “learns” from existing information that is programmed within the system. This allows the machine to facilitate cognitive functions that are usually associated with human minds and subsequent thinking.

The 7 Main Types of Artifical Intelligence

The Creation Process

Narrow AI or Also Known As ANI

Narrow AI is any kind of artificial intelligence system and/or form of AI technology that's sole purpose is to complete a set of very specific actions or basic commands. While ANI's are created with the intended goal in mind of serving and exceling in a cognitive capability, this particular sub-category of AI technologies still aren't able to independently learn new skills that it's not already programmed and/or wasn't intially designed to learn. These kind of technologies are developed by combining a subfield of artifical intelligence called "machine learning" with neural network algorithms to fucntion properly and accomplish the specificed tasks it's programmed to complete. Through machine learning, AI systems are provided with the ability to process data and statistics, while simultaneously applying problem-solving strategies that are similar to that of strategies used by actual people, like that of trail and error, to improve it's own processes and "think" at faster rates. Examples of such A.I. include image recognition software, self-driving cars, and AI virtual assistants.


Artifical Genral Intelligence (AGI)

Artificial General Intelligence and/or general AI is any form of artificial intelligence that's capacity for thinking, learning, and preforming more closely resembles that of a real person, as it's able to preform more divesive and detailed directions/tasks. While AGI's are still a working progress, the fundamental framework for this type of AI may be constructed from technologies such as supercomputers, quantum hardware and generative AI models like that of ChatGPT.

Artificial Superintelligence

Artificial Superintelligence and/or super AI is more of a figment of the imgaination and is showcased through numerous science fiction movies like that of The Termninator and Machina. It's the infamously known theroy that artificial intelligence will become significantly more knowledgable and efficient than humans, eventually becoming the backbone of another sub-genre of AI called self-aware AI and other individualistic, robotic projects. As CEO of the AI writing company, Jasper, Dave Rogenmoser once said himself,"Artificial superintelligence will become by far the most capable forms of intelligence on earth.... It will have the intelligence of human beings and will be exceedingly better at everything that we do."
